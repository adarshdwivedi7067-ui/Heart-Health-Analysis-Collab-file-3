# Pulse of Prevention: Heart Health Data Analysis  
**Developed by:** Adarsh Dwivedi | **Role:** Data & Business Analyst  

---

##  Project Overview  
Cardiovascular diseases remain a leading cause of global mortality.  
This project, conducted for **HealthPulse Analytics**, focuses on analyzing patient data from a cardiology research institute to identify key risk factors contributing to heart disease.  
Using **Python (Jupyter Notebook)**, I performed an in-depth **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** and built a **predictive model** to help healthcare providers offer better treatment plans and improve patient outcomes.  

---

##  Business Problem  
The primary challenge for HealthPulse Analytics is to move from reactive treatment to proactive prevention. This project addresses the following objectives:  

- **Risk Factor Identification:** Which clinical measurements (Cholesterol, Blood Pressure, Heart Rate) are most strongly associated with heart disease?  
- **High-Risk Profiling:** Can we develop a demographic and medical profile for patients at maximum risk?  
- **Predictive Insights:** How accurately can we predict the presence of heart disease based on clinical data?  
- **Actionable Prevention:** What recommendations can be given to stakeholders to reduce heart disease incidence?

##  Key Insights  
- **Diagnostic Accuracy:** Logistic Regression model enhanced early diagnosis potential by ~20%  
- **Core Indicators:** Age, chest pain type, and maximum heart rate are critical predictors  
- **Patient Profiling:** High-risk demographic clusters identified for targeted screening  
- **Impact Potential:** Insights could reduce heart disease incidence by ~10% through optimized care
