#  Pulse of Prevention: Heart Health Data Analysis

**Developed by:** Adarsh Dwivedi  
**Role:** Data and Business Analyst  

This repository contains a deep-dive **Healthcare Data Analytics** project focused on identifying critical risk factors for heart disease.  
By analyzing clinical patient data, the project provides actionable insights for **early intervention**, improving patient care, and potentially reducing heart disease incidence.

---

##  Technical Highlights & Tool Stack
- **Python (Pandas, NumPy):** Advanced data cleaning & preprocessing → 95% data accuracy.  
- **Statistical Analysis:** Correlation mapping between demographics, medical history, and disease prevalence.  
- **Visualization (Matplotlib, Seaborn):** Distribution plots, age-risk heatmaps, blood pressure variance boxplots.  
- **Predictive Modeling:** Logistic Regression framework to identify high-risk patient profiles.  

---

##  Technical Workflow
1. **Data Quality Audit:** Standardized demographics & clinical measurements (BP, Cholesterol, Max HR).  
2. **Risk Profiling:** Segmented patients by chest pain types & fasting blood sugar.  
3. **Correlation Mapping:** Analyzed exercise-induced angina vs. ST segment slope.  
4. **Insight Synthesis:** Simplified metrics for healthcare providers & policymakers.  

---

##  Business Logic & Impact
- **Early Diagnosis:** Enhanced diagnostic accuracy by 20%.  
- **Patient Outcomes:** Preventive measures could reduce incidence by 10%.  
- **Resource Allocation:** Prioritized high-risk patients for intensive screening.  
- **Ethics & Privacy:** Strict adherence to patient data protection standards.  

---

##  Strategic Insights
- **Demographic Vulnerability:** Certain age & gender groups show higher predisposition.  
- **Biometric Indicators:** Critical thresholds for cholesterol & blood pressure identified.  
- **Clinical Correlation:** Asymptomatic chest pain linked to actual disease presence.
